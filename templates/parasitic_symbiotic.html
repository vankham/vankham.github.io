PARASITIC | SYMBIOTIC

After completing site analysis for two different sites on campus, we came up with a small scale design. This design would remain the same and be parasitic to one site and symbiotic to the other. My concept ‘Journey: Into the Light’ was formed from one of my favorite sea caves in San Diego. I wanted to create a space that has a small and enclosed entry space which would then open up to an open seating and gathering area with water features to mimic the sound of the ocean.

For the final portion of the project we took our small scale design and then expanded it to fit the greater context of our site while still reflecting our concept. I decided to create a symbiotic landscape rather than a parasitic one. My final site location was the plaza between Baker Science and the Science Building. Throughout the design there are many coastal plants as well as a viewing deck of a sloping grass hill to reflect the vast open ocean.
